Welcome to Domenic's Cybersecurity & OSINT Services

OSINT Testing and Threat Modeling Services

I specialized in identifying security risks through open-source intelligence (OSINT) and helped businesses protect themselves from potential threats. My services included:

  • OSINT Audits: Discover publicly accessible information that may put your business at risk.
  • Threat Modeling: Develop security strategies based on collected intelligence.
  • Social Engineering Defense: Simulations and training to defend against phishing and impersonation attacks.

Notable Projects

Mock OSINT Report on Experian

Conducted a detailed OSINT investigation on Experian, identifying potential security and privacy risks from publicly available information. Read the full Experian OSINT Report to learn more.

Web Application Fuzzer with GUI (QT6 Framework)

Developed a fuzzer tool for web applications with a user-friendly graphical interface, designed to identify vulnerabilities through automated testing.

Metadata Analyzer (Python)

ChatCSEC - Undergraduate Capstone Project

Developed ChatCSEC, an LLM-based chatbot for computing security education, as part of a group project. Collaborated with a team to design, develop, and deploy the chatbot.

Vulnerabilities in Tap-To-Pay Systems

Participated in a research project analyzing vulnerabilities in Tap-To-Pay systems. Documented potential security flaws and contributed to a comprehensive research paper.

Cuckoo Watchtower

Created Cuckoo Watchtower to assist with malware forensics by integrating GPT-4 to analyze Cuckoo Sandbox JSON reports. Enhanced malware analysis with interactive, chatbot-style insights within a Google Colab environment. The tool provided dynamic reporting, interactive querying, and advanced search capabilities using vector databases.

AudioCovert

Developed AudioCovert, a tool to embed and decode hidden messages in audio files. Encoded messages into WAV files, manipulating high-frequency signals to make them imperceptible to the human ear. Messages were decoded by generating and inspecting the audio file's spectrogram.
