Comprehensive OSINT Testing and Threat Modeling Services

I provide organizations with expert OSINT services to identify potential security vulnerabilities through publicly available information. Whether you're looking to safeguard your organization against social engineering or need a threat model built to mitigate risks, I can assist in designing and implementing security strategies that suit your business needs.

Our Service Offerings

  • OSINT Audits: A thorough review of publicly available information about your organization to assess risks of exposure, including key individuals, infrastructure, and sensitive data leaks.
  • Threat Modeling: Analyze your business processes and digital footprint to develop tailored threat models that simulate potential attack vectors and security breaches.
  • Social Engineering Simulations: Train your staff with phishing and impersonation simulations to prepare them for real-world social engineering attacks.
  • Digital Footprint Reduction: Help individuals within your organization reduce their online presence to minimize risks posed by OSINT techniques.

Our Methodology

We follow a structured and precise methodology to ensure that our findings are comprehensive and actionable. Here’s how we approach OSINT testing and threat modeling:

  1. Discovery Phase: We start by collecting all relevant publicly available information about your organization using OSINT techniques, from social media profiles to domain records and network infrastructure.
  2. Risk Analysis: After gathering the data, we assess the likelihood of these exposed elements being used in an attack, focusing on key areas such as social engineering and infrastructure vulnerabilities.
  3. Threat Modeling: Based on the gathered intelligence, we create detailed models simulating potential threats that your organization might face and provide solutions to mitigate these risks.
  4. Reporting & Recommendations: We provide a detailed report summarizing our findings, with clear steps for remediation and risk mitigation.

Case Study: Experian OSINT Report

In a mock investigation for Experian, I conducted an OSINT analysis that uncovered potential vulnerabilities in both organizational and individual-level data. Here’s an overview of the key findings:

  • Organizational Exposure: The public availability of social media profiles and network infrastructure information elevated the risk of phishing attacks targeting Experian customers.
  • Individual Risks: Personal details about key directors, including contact information and family details, were accessible through OSINT techniques, increasing the risk of social engineering attacks against high-ranking individuals.
  • Recommendations: I advised Experian to implement strict digital footprint reduction measures, provide training for key employees, and remove sensitive data from public platforms where possible.
